ACTION & REACTION: ANATOMY OF THE CARCERAL STATE


VIEW THE TRANSCRIPTS


13 ARTISTS HAVE BEEN PAIRED WITH 13 INCARCERATED PEOPLE FOR, “ACTION AND REACTION: ANATOMY OF THE CARCERAL STATE.” A BOOK OF PERSONAL STORIES AND REACTION ART. EACH STORY HIGHLIGHTS A SPECIFIC ASPECT OF LIFE INCARCERATED. WHEN COMPILED TOGETHER, THEY TELL A LARGER STORY OF THE HUMAN RIGHTS VIOLATIONS AND DEVASTATION PERPETRATED BY MASS INCARCERATION.

*PROCEEDS WILL GO TO THE INCARCERATED INDIVIDUALS FEATURED IN THE BOOK.
