Flex on Agro Complex

Martin Kačmarek

Acrylic and Oil on Canvas

90 x 90 cm

About the Work :: Martin Kačmarek's work is inpsired by shapes, colours, textures and principles of randomness. Recently, he has embraced his natural fascination with the world of agriculture and DIY culture. Kačmarek’s monumental paintings are distinguished by an unconstrained painterly signature-style, which suppresses the ordinary aesthetic norms. Formless bodies and absurd portraits of fictive farmers result from his flat shapes and simple shading, mixed with humour, satire, irony, and a surrealist construction of the pictorial narrative.
