Gold Standard

Anthony Eslick

Acrylic on Panel

36 x 24 in

About the Work :: Lodged between the mainstream commercial artwork from his childhood, and the digital art landscape he works in today, Anthony uses images of violence, humor, cultural iconography, longing, isolation and horror to guide the narrative of his paintings. Often taking queues from scribbled notes and dreams, his process sits somewhere between the complacency of nostalgia and the spastic immediacy of the moment.
