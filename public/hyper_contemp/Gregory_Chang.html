Gregory

Niko Chang

About the Work :: "Gregory" by Niko Chang is a digital piece done in response to a letter from our incarcerated colleague, Gregory, on his experience in the United States prison system. 13 incarcerated individuals were paired with 13 artists who created art in response to the stories they received. "Gregory" is the only piece to be minted.
