Martin Kačmarek

Acrylic and Oil on Canvas

99 x 99 cm

About the Work :: Shapes, colours and textures fascinate him, and he also in large measure takes account of the principle of randomness. In a series of recent paintings he has turned aside from the digital medium and computer games as inspirations and gradually moved on to his natural fascination with the world of agriculture and DIY culture. Kačmarek’s monumental paintings are distinguished by an unconstrained painterly signature-style, which suppresses the ordinary aesthetic norms. Formless bodies and absurd portraits of fictive farmers result from his flat shapes and simple shading, mixed with humour, satire, irony, and a surrealist construction of the pictorial narrative.
