It's Raining Scissors

Attilo Esposito

Oil on Canvas

100 x 65 in

“I create indefinite shapes with plasticine, take photos and then group them digitally in aesthetically pleasing compositions before bringing it to canvas. My work does not have a meaningful objective reading, as today, I find interest in the process by which images are compiled. In general, everything only receives a true meaning only through the observer. My titles are then my personal interaction with the image.”
